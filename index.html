Just say "Hey Jarvis"

Always listening for its name, never recording until you call it. Speak naturally — no commands to memorize, no app to open.

STEP 03 · ACT

It does the work

Devices, apps, email, calendar, the web — Jarvis takes the real-world actions a sharp assistant would, in a second or two.

It learns your world

Every interaction makes the next one better. Sensitive actions can pause for your okay, and everything stays on your terms.
